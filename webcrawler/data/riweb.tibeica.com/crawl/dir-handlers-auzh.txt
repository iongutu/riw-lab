  <h2><a name="SECTION007180000000000000000">&nbsp;</a> <a name="l2h-245">&nbsp;</a> <br> 5.1.8 PythonAuthzHandler </h2> 
  <p> <b><em class="citetitle"><a href="http://httpd.apache.org/docs-2.0/mod/directive-dict.html#Syntax" title="Syntax:">Syntax:</a></em></b> <i>Python*Handler Syntax</i> <br><em class="citetitle"><a href="http://httpd.apache.org/docs-2.0/mod/directive-dict.html#Context" title="Context:">Context:</a></em> server config, virtual host, directory, htaccess <br><em class="citetitle"><a href="http://httpd.apache.org/docs-2.0/mod/directive-dict.html#Override" title="Override:">Override:</a></em> not None <br><em class="citetitle"><a href="http://httpd.apache.org/docs-2.0/mod/directive-dict.html#Module" title="Module:">Module:</a></em> mod_python.c </p>
